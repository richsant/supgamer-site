How Users Consent to Receive SMS

During account registration in the supGamer mobile app, users voluntarily provide their phone number. Upon entering their phone number and requesting a verification code, users consent to receive a one-time SMS verification code (OTP) to confirm their identity. No marketing, promotional, or recurring messages are sent. SMS is used exclusively for authentication purposes.

Message Details

Message frequency: One-time per login or registration attempt.

Sample message: "Your supGamer verification code is: 123456"

Opt-out: Users can stop receiving SMS verification codes by not requesting a code or by deleting their account.
